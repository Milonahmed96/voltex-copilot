This document explains your rights when returning a product to Voltex, how our repair
process works, and what you are entitled to when things go wrong. We have written this
to be understood by customers and used as a reference by our colleagues. Your statutory
rights under the Consumer Rights Act 2015 are explained clearly in Part 1 — we will
never try to offer you less than the law entitles you to.

PART 1 — YOUR STATUTORY RIGHTS (WHAT THE LAW GIVES YOU)
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

Under the Consumer Rights Act 2015, you have the following rights when a product you
buy from Voltex turns out to be faulty, not as described, or not fit for purpose.
These rights exist regardless of whether you have VoltCare or any other protection plan.

WITHIN 30 DAYS OF PURCHASE — THE SHORT-TERM RIGHT TO REJECT

If your product develops a fault within the first 30 days, you have the right to a full
refund. You do not have to accept a repair or replacement if you do not want one, although
you may choose either if you prefer.

What counts as a fault: a product is faulty if it does not work as it should through
normal use, if it breaks down without misuse or accidental damage, or if it does not
match what was described to you at the point of sale.

What does not count as a fault for the purpose of this right:
- Damage caused by accidental impact, liquid, or misuse
- A product you have changed your mind about (see Part 2 for our goodwill returns policy)
- Software you have installed that has caused problems
- Normal wear to consumable parts (see VoltCare policy for details)

The 30-day clock: starts on the day you receive your product (for online orders, this
is the delivery date; for in-store purchases, it is the purchase date). If a fault
appears on day 30, you are still within the window. If it appears on day 31, you move
to the 6-month rules below.

BETWEEN 30 DAYS AND 6 MONTHS — REPAIR OR REPLACEMENT FIRST

If a fault appears between day 31 and day 180 of ownership, Voltex must repair or
replace the product at no cost to you. During this period the law assumes the fault
was present from the start — you do not need to prove this.

The sequence works as follows:
First, Voltex will offer a repair or replacement. We choose which to offer first,
but we must not offer one that is significantly more inconvenient for you than the other.
If the repair or replacement fails, or if we cannot do it within a reasonable time and
without significant inconvenience to you, you are then entitled to a price reduction
(keeping the product) or a full refund.
A repair that fails means the same fault recurs. A second attempt may be made, but if
the fault appears a third time, you can immediately claim a refund or price reduction
without any further repair attempts.

AFTER 6 MONTHS — YOUR RIGHTS CONTINUE BUT THE BURDEN SHIFTS

Your statutory rights do not end at 6 months. They continue for up to 6 years from
purchase in England, Wales, and Northern Ireland (5 years in Scotland).
However, after 6 months, the burden of proof shifts to you. You may need to show that
the fault was present at the time of purchase — for example, by obtaining an independent
engineer's report. If you have VoltCare, making a claim under your plan is usually more
straightforward than pursuing a statutory claim at this stage, though both options
remain available to you.

IMPORTANT FOR AGENTS: do not steer customers toward VoltCare if their statutory rights
give them a stronger remedy. Always explain both options. If a customer is within 30 days
and wants a refund, process a refund — do not initiate a VoltCare claim instead.

PART 2 — VOLTEX GOODWILL RETURNS (CHANGE OF MIND)
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

Your statutory rights cover faulty products. Voltex also offers a goodwill returns policy
for customers who simply change their mind — this is a voluntary policy and goes beyond
what the law requires.

STANDARD GOODWILL RETURNS WINDOW: 30 DAYS

You may return most products within 30 days of purchase for any reason, provided:
- The product is in its original condition and packaging
- All accessories, manuals, and included items are present
- The product has not been registered with the manufacturer using your personal details
  (some manufacturers lock devices to an account — if this has happened, the product
  cannot be returned to us as new)
- For software and digital content: not opened or downloaded (once opened, digital
  content cannot be returned under our goodwill policy, though statutory rights for
  faulty digital content still apply)

Products that cannot be returned under our goodwill policy regardless of condition:
- Personal hygiene products (electric shavers, epilators, earbuds that have been removed
  from sealed packaging) due to hygiene reasons
- Custom-configured products (laptops or desktops built to your specification)
- Products with the tamper-evident seal broken where the seal is integral to the product
  (some software, some accessories)
- Consumable items that have been partially used

EXTENDED GOODWILL RETURNS AT CHRISTMAS

Products purchased between 1 November and 24 December can be returned for any reason
up to and including 31 January of the following year, provided the above conditions are met.
This applies to gifts — the recipient may return the item without the original purchaser
being present, provided they have the receipt or order confirmation.

ONLINE DISTANCE SELLING REGULATIONS — 14-DAY CANCELLATION RIGHT

If you purchased online or by phone (distance selling), you have an additional statutory
right to cancel your order within 14 days of receiving your product for any reason,
without needing to give a reason. This right exists under the Consumer Contracts Regulations
2013 and is separate from our goodwill returns window.

Under this right:
- You must notify us of cancellation within 14 days of delivery
- You then have a further 14 days to return the product to us
- We refund within 14 days of receiving the product back
- You are responsible for return postage costs unless the product is faulty
- We may reduce your refund if the product has been used beyond what is necessary to
  inspect it (i.e. more than you would do in a shop)

This right does NOT apply to: in-store purchases, personalised or custom-built products,
sealed audio/video recordings or software once unsealed, and digital content once
downloading has begun with your agreement.

PART 3 — HOW TO RETURN A PRODUCT
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

SMALL AND MEDIUM PRODUCTS (LAPTOPS, PHONES, TABLETS, AUDIO, GAMING, SMALL APPLIANCES)

In store: bring the product, its original packaging, all accessories, and your proof of
purchase to any Voltex store. A colleague will inspect the product and process your return
or exchange. Returns are typically processed in under 15 minutes.

By post (online purchases only): log in to voltex.co.uk/my-orders, select the item,
and choose Return. Print the prepaid return label (we cover return postage for faulty
products; for change-of-mind returns, a £4.99 postage fee is deducted from your refund).
Pack the product securely in its original packaging. Drop it at any Royal Mail or Evri
collection point shown in your return confirmation.

LARGE PRODUCTS (TELEVISIONS OVER 43 INCHES, WHITE GOODS, AMERICAN-STYLE FRIDGE-FREEZERS)

Large products cannot be returned via post or brought to a store. Instead:
Call 0333 400 7000 (option 3) or start a return at voltex.co.uk/returns to book a
large item collection. We will collect the product from your home, typically within
5 business days of your request.

For white goods that have been installed (plumbed in, built in, or hardwired):
We do not disconnect or uninstall appliances as part of a standard collection. If your
appliance needs disconnection, you will need to arrange this before our collection visit,
or purchase a VoltInstall disconnection service (from £29). This applies to faulty and
change-of-mind returns.
Exception: if the appliance is faulty and you are within your 30-day statutory right
to reject, we will arrange disconnection at no charge as part of our collection service.

WHAT TO DO BEFORE RETURNING ANY DEVICE WITH PERSONAL DATA

Before returning a phone, tablet, laptop, or any device that stores personal information:
Remove your accounts (Apple ID, Google account, Microsoft account) and perform a factory reset.
Back up any data you wish to keep — Voltex cannot recover data after a factory reset.
For phones: remove your SIM card and any memory cards.
Remove all screen protectors and cases (accessories are not part of the return unless they
were included in the original product box).

If you are unable to factory reset a faulty device (because it will not turn on), tell our
colleagues at the point of return or on the phone. Our repair team will securely wipe
all data before any work is carried out.

PROOF OF PURCHASE

A receipt, order confirmation email, or transaction visible in your Voltex online account
all constitute valid proof of purchase. We can also look up purchases using the card
you paid with, provided the transaction is under 3 years old.
We do not require the original physical receipt — digital proof is accepted.
Without any form of proof of purchase, we may be unable to process a return. In this case,
please speak to a store manager or call our customer relations team — we handle these
situations case by case.

PART 4 — REFUNDS: HOW AND WHEN YOU RECEIVE YOUR MONEY
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

REFUND METHOD

Your refund is returned using the same method you used to pay. This is a legal requirement
and cannot be changed to a different method without your consent.

Paid by debit or credit card: refunded to the same card. Allow 3 to 5 business days
for the refund to appear, depending on your bank. We process refunds same day — the
delay is on your bank's side, not ours.

Paid by Voltex gift card: refunded as a new Voltex gift card to the same value.

Paid by VoltPay (Voltex finance): your finance agreement is adjusted or cancelled
depending on whether you paid partly or fully by finance. See Part 5 for full details
on finance refunds.

Paid using a combination of methods (e.g. part gift card, part card): each element
is refunded separately to the original payment method.

Cash payments (in-store): refunded in cash at the store where you make the return.
If the store does not hold sufficient cash, a same-day bank transfer can be arranged
— speak to the store manager.

REFUND TIMESCALES

In-store returns: card refunds processed immediately at the till; typically visible
in your account within 3 to 5 business days.
Postal returns: refund processed within 5 business days of us receiving the product.
Large item collections: refund processed within 3 business days of our collection team
confirming receipt and condition of the product.

If you have not received your refund within 10 business days of it being processed,
please contact us. We will provide the refund transaction reference so you can trace
it with your bank.

PARTIAL REFUNDS

A partial refund may be issued in the following circumstances:
Change-of-mind return where the product shows signs of use beyond normal inspection
(at our reasonable discretion — we will explain any deduction clearly).
Return of a bundle where one component is missing (the missing item's retail value
is deducted from the refund).
Return of a product where a promotional discount was applied that is no longer valid
(e.g. a buy-one-get-one promotion where only one item is returned).

PART 5 — VOLTPAY FINANCE RETURNS AND REFUNDS
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

VoltPay is Voltex's Buy Now Pay Later and interest-free credit service, provided in
partnership with Duologi Finance Ltd (FCA authorised). Returns on VoltPay purchases
work slightly differently from standard card refunds.

FULL RETURN WITHIN 30 DAYS (NO PAYMENTS MADE YET)

If you return a product in full before any payment has been made or fallen due, the
finance agreement is cancelled and you owe nothing. No refund is issued to a bank
account as no money has yet changed hands.

PARTIAL PAYMENTS MADE

If you have made one or more VoltPay payments before returning the product:
The finance agreement is cancelled.
Voltex refunds the full product value to Duologi.
Duologi refunds any payments you have already made to your original payment method.
Allow up to 10 business days for this process to complete.
You will receive a cancellation confirmation from Duologi by email.

PARTIAL RETURN ON A FINANCE ORDER (KEEPING SOME ITEMS)

If you purchased multiple items on one VoltPay agreement and return only some of them:
The finance agreement is recalculated to reflect only the items you are keeping.
Your monthly payment and/or deferred balance is reduced accordingly.
Duologi will send you an updated agreement by email within 5 business days.

EARLY SETTLEMENT

If you wish to pay off your VoltPay balance early (before the interest-free period ends):
Log in to your Duologi account at duologi.voltex.co.uk or call 0333 400 8000.
There are no early settlement charges on any Voltex VoltPay agreement.

REFUNDS AFTER THE INTEREST-FREE PERIOD HAS ENDED

If you have held a product past the interest-free period and interest has accrued on
your Duologi account, the refund for the product value is returned to Duologi. You
remain responsible for any interest charges already accrued. If you believe interest
has been charged in error, contact Duologi directly.

PART 6 — REPAIRS OUTSIDE OF VOLTCARE
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

If you do not have VoltCare, or if your VoltCare claim has been declined, you still
have the following repair options.

MANUFACTURER WARRANTY REPAIRS

Most products come with a manufacturer's warranty — typically 12 months, though some
manufacturers offer 2 or 3 years (Samsung, Apple, and LG offer extended warranties on
selected products). During the manufacturer warranty period, the manufacturer will
repair or replace a faulty product at no charge.

Voltex can facilitate manufacturer warranty repairs for most brands. Bring your product
to any Voltex store and we will log it with the manufacturer on your behalf. This is
a free service. Timescales vary by manufacturer — we will give you an estimate at the
point of logging.

If the manufacturer's warranty has expired, you may still have statutory rights under
the Consumer Rights Act (see Part 1). We can also offer a paid repair — see below.

PAID REPAIRS (OUT OF WARRANTY, NO VOLTCARE)

Voltex offers paid repairs for most products through our Newark Repair Centre.

How paid repairs work:
Step 1 — Diagnostic: bring your product to a Voltex store or request a collection
(£9.99 collection fee applies). The repair centre will diagnose the fault and provide
a fixed repair quote.
Step 2 — You accept or decline: you will receive the quote by email and SMS.
You have 7 days to accept. If you decline, we return your product unrepaired.
Collection fee is non-refundable if you decline the repair quote.
Step 3 — Repair: if you accept the quote, we carry out the repair and return the product.
Paid repair timescale: 5 to 10 business days depending on parts availability.

Paid repair guarantee: all paid repairs carry a 90-day repair guarantee. If the same
fault recurs within 90 days of a paid repair, we will repair it again at no charge.
This guarantee covers the specific fault repaired — it is not a general product warranty.

Products we do not repair on a paid basis:
- Products where parts are no longer manufactured or available
- Products where the repair cost would exceed 70% of the product's current replacement value
  (we will advise you of this at diagnostic stage)
- Products with evidence of liquid damage where corrosion has spread to the main board
  (assessment-dependent; we will advise)

PHONE SCREEN REPLACEMENTS AT TECH BARS

47 Voltex stores have a Tech Bar where we carry out phone screen replacements while
you wait, typically in 60 to 90 minutes. Prices vary by model — check voltex.co.uk/techbar
or call your local store for a quote.

Tech Bar screen replacements use Voltex-certified parts. They carry the same 90-day
repair guarantee as all paid repairs. Tech Bar repairs do not affect your manufacturer
warranty on other components (we use non-manufacturer parts but the repair process
is certified).

For iPhone screen replacements: Voltex is an Apple Authorised Service Provider at
selected stores. Apple-authorised repairs use genuine Apple parts and preserve your
Apple warranty in full. Check voltex.co.uk/apple-repair for participating stores.

PART 7 — EXCHANGES
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

LIKE-FOR-LIKE EXCHANGE

If your product is faulty and you would prefer an exchange to a refund:
Within 30 days: we will exchange for the same product where stock is available.
If the same product is not available: we will offer an equivalent alternative of
equal or higher specification, or a full refund — your choice.
We cannot offer an exchange of lower specification without your agreement, even if
the lower-specification product is available immediately and the correct one requires
a wait.

UPGRADE EXCHANGE (PAY THE DIFFERENCE)

If you are returning a faulty product and would like to upgrade to a different model,
you may exchange and pay the price difference. This is processed as a return of the
original product (full refund) followed by a new purchase.

If paying by VoltPay: a new finance agreement is required for the upgraded product.
The original agreement is cancelled as part of the return.

CHANGE-OF-MIND EXCHANGES

If you are returning a product under our goodwill policy (Part 2) and want a different
product rather than a refund, we will process this as a return and new purchase.
Any price difference is collected or refunded at the point of exchange.

PART 8 — MARKETPLACE AND THIRD-PARTY SELLER RETURNS
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

Some products on voltex.co.uk are sold by third-party sellers through the Voltex
Marketplace. These are clearly labelled "Sold by [Seller Name], fulfilled by Voltex"
or "Sold and dispatched by [Seller Name]" on the product page and in your order confirmation.

YOUR RIGHTS ON MARKETPLACE PURCHASES

Your statutory rights under the Consumer Rights Act still apply — you have the same
faulty product rights as with any Voltex-sold item.

However, our goodwill returns policy is the seller's responsibility, not Voltex's.
Marketplace sellers must offer a minimum 14-day change-of-mind return under the Consumer
Contracts Regulations, but they are not obligated to offer Voltex's 30-day goodwill window.
The seller's returns policy is shown on their seller profile page.

HOW TO RETURN A MARKETPLACE ITEM

Log in to voltex.co.uk/my-orders and select the marketplace order.
For faulty items: Voltex mediates. If the seller does not resolve a faulty item
return within 5 business days, contact Voltex Customer Services and we will escalate
on your behalf. As a last resort, Voltex's Marketplace Guarantee ensures you will
receive a refund for any faulty product regardless of seller cooperation.
For change-of-mind: follow the seller's stated returns process.

VOLTCARE ON MARKETPLACE PRODUCTS

VoltCare is not available on marketplace products sold by third-party sellers.
Only products sold directly by Voltex (labelled "Sold by Voltex") are eligible for VoltCare.

PART 9 — EDGE CASES AND SITUATIONS AGENTS ARE FREQUENTLY ASKED ABOUT
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

"I bought this as a gift and the recipient wants to return it but doesn't have the receipt"
The gift recipient can return the product within our goodwill window using the original
purchaser's proof of purchase. If the original purchaser has a Voltex account, we can
look up the transaction. If the item was purchased in cash with no account, we may not
be able to locate the transaction — speak to a store manager who can review case by case.
Refund is issued to the original payment method (back to the purchaser's card or account),
not to the recipient, unless a gift receipt was issued (see below).

Voltex gift receipts: when a customer marks an order as a gift, a gift receipt is generated.
Gift receipts entitle the recipient to an exchange or store credit to the product value.
They do not entitle the recipient to a cash refund to a card they don't hold.

"The product worked fine in the store but stopped working when I got it home"
This is a fault. If within 30 days, process as a statutory 30-day rejection — full refund,
or repair/replacement at customer's choice. Do not suggest the customer "must have caused it"
without evidence. The burden of proof in the first 6 months is on Voltex, not the customer.

"I've had this TV for 14 months and the screen has developed a fault — my warranty is up"
The manufacturer warranty may be expired, but statutory rights continue for up to 6 years.
After 6 months the burden of proof shifts to the customer, but a 14-month-old TV with a
screen fault is very likely to be a manufacturing defect. If the customer has VoltCare,
process as a VoltCare claim. If not, explain that we can facilitate a manufacturer goodwill
repair request (many manufacturers will repair outside warranty for demonstrably premature
faults) or offer a paid repair quote. Do not simply tell the customer "your warranty has expired."

"I dropped my laptop and the screen cracked — can I return it?"
Accidental damage is not a fault and does not trigger statutory return rights. If the customer
has VoltCare Plus or Complete, this is a VoltCare accidental damage claim, not a return.
If they do not have VoltCare, this is a paid repair situation. Do not process as a standard return.

"I bought a laptop online, opened it, used it for a week to see if I liked it, now I want to return it"
Under the Consumer Contracts Regulations, customers may use the product to the extent
necessary to inspect it — as they would in a shop. Using a laptop for a week of actual work
goes beyond inspection. We may apply a deduction for use before refunding. The deduction is
assessed at the discretion of the Customer Relations team and is based on the extent of use,
not a fixed formula. Agents should not promise a full refund in this scenario — log the
return request and pass to Customer Relations for assessment.

"I want to return my washing machine but it's already been plumbed in"
Change-of-mind return: the customer is responsible for disconnection before collection.
We can offer VoltInstall disconnection at a fee. The product must be in returnable condition
(no damage beyond installation marks). Faulty product return within 30 days: we arrange
disconnection as part of collection at no charge.

"The product I bought was described as having a feature it doesn't actually have"
This is a "not as described" situation, which is a statutory right regardless of the time
elapsed since purchase. If the product description on voltex.co.uk or on in-store signage
included a feature the product does not have, the customer is entitled to a full refund
or replacement at any point. Escalate to Team Leader and flag for the Trading Standards team.
Do not ask the customer to prove the misdescription — check the product listing yourself
in the CRM product catalogue.
