This document explains how Voltex delivers products, how to manage your order after
placing it, and what to do when something goes wrong. Delivery is one of the most
important parts of your experience with us — if anything is unclear or does not go
to plan, call us on 0333 400 7000 (option 1 for orders and delivery) or visit any store.

PART 1 — DELIVERY OPTIONS AND TIMESCALES
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

Voltex uses two entirely separate delivery systems depending on the size and type
of product you have ordered. It is important to understand which system your order
is using, as the booking process, timescales, and what happens on delivery day
are completely different.

SMALL ITEM DELIVERY (courier)

Used for: phones, tablets, laptops, audio equipment, gaming consoles and accessories,
small domestic appliances (kettles, toasters, coffee machines, air fryers), accessories,
and any product that can be safely handled by one person and fits in a standard parcel.

Delivery partners: Voltex uses Evri, DPD, and Royal Mail for small item delivery
depending on parcel size, postcode, and carrier availability at the time of despatch.
You will be told which carrier is handling your delivery in your despatch confirmation email.

Standard delivery: free on orders over £25. £3.99 on orders under £25.
Target timescale: 2 to 4 business days from order confirmation.
Named day delivery: available for an additional £2.99. Select your preferred delivery
date at checkout. Available Monday to Saturday, subject to carrier availability in your area.
Next-day delivery: available on orders placed before 10pm for an additional £5.99.
Subject to stock availability and carrier cut-off times. Not available on all postcodes
(see Part 1.4 for postcode restrictions).
Sunday delivery: available in selected postcodes via DPD for an additional £4.99.
Check availability at checkout.

Delivery time window: standard courier deliveries do not come with a pre-booked time slot.
Carriers provide a 1-hour estimated arrival window on the morning of delivery by SMS
and email. This window is an estimate — actual delivery may fall slightly outside it.
Named day delivery includes a 1-hour window. Next-day does not guarantee a specific time.

LARGE ITEM DELIVERY (specialist two-person team)

Used for: televisions 43 inches and above, all white goods (washing machines,
fridge-freezers, dishwashers, ovens, range cookers, tumble dryers), large audio
equipment (floorstanding speakers), and any product that requires two people to carry safely.

Delivery partner: Voltex operates its own large item delivery fleet, supplemented
by certified third-party haulage partners in areas outside our direct fleet coverage.
Unlike courier delivery, all large item deliveries are fully tracked and handled by
trained Voltex-certified delivery teams.

Large item delivery: included in the product price on all large items. No additional charge.
Timescale: a delivery date is selected at checkout from available slots. Typical lead
time is 3 to 7 business days, though this varies by postcode and product availability.
If no slots are shown at checkout within 7 days, the next available slot will be shown —
during peak periods (Black Friday, January sales, bank holidays) slots may be 10 to 14
business days out.
Delivery time slot: a 3-hour delivery window is provided the evening before delivery
by SMS and email. A narrower 1-hour window is sent on the morning of delivery.
Saturday delivery: available for large items at no extra charge, subject to slot availability.
Sunday and bank holiday delivery: not available for large items.

SAME-DAY DELIVERY

Available on selected small items in selected postcodes (primarily major UK cities).
Check eligibility at checkout — the same-day option will appear if available for your
postcode and the product you are ordering.
Orders must be placed before 1pm for same-day delivery.
Same-day delivery is carried out by Stuart, a local courier partner.
Additional charge: £9.99.
Same-day delivery is not available for large items under any circumstances.

PART 2 — CLICK AND COLLECT
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

Click and collect allows you to order online and collect from your chosen Voltex store.
This is free for all orders regardless of value.

STANDARD CLICK AND COLLECT

If the product is in stock at your chosen store: typically ready for collection within
2 hours of order confirmation. You will receive a "Ready to collect" SMS and email —
do not travel to the store before receiving this notification, even if the timescale
suggests the item should be ready.
If the product needs to be transferred from another location: timescale is 1 to 3
business days. You will receive collection confirmation when the item arrives at your store.

WHAT TO BRING WHEN COLLECTING
The order confirmation email or the Voltex app with your order visible.
A form of photo ID (driving licence or passport) if collecting on behalf of someone else
or if the order was paid by finance.
The payment card used to place the order — for security purposes, the card may be
checked on collection for orders over £200.

COLLECTION TIME LIMIT
Your order will be held at the store for 7 days from the "Ready to collect" notification.
If you do not collect within 7 days, the order is cancelled and refunded in full.
If you need more time, call the store directly or contact us on 0333 400 7000 — extensions
can be granted at store manager discretion, typically up to an additional 7 days.

LARGE ITEM CLICK AND COLLECT
White goods and large TVs can be collected from store in most cases, but:
Not all stores have the warehouse space to hold large appliances — check at checkout
whether large item collection is available at your chosen store.
You must have appropriate transport for the product. Voltex colleagues will assist
loading into your vehicle but cannot transport the product for you.
If you are collecting a large item, bring a vehicle large enough to transport it safely.
Voltex is not responsible for damage caused by the customer's own transport arrangements.

PART 3 — LARGE ITEM DELIVERY: WHAT HAPPENS ON DELIVERY DAY
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

Large item delivery involves more than dropping a parcel at the door. This section
explains exactly what the delivery team will and will not do, so there are no
surprises on delivery day.

STANDARD LARGE ITEM DELIVERY (INCLUDED IN PRODUCT PRICE)

Our delivery team will:
- Deliver the product to the room of your choice (ground floor or first floor only
  as standard — see note on upper floors below)
- Unpack the product and remove all packaging
- Position the product in your chosen location
- Carry out a basic functionality check (power on, confirm the product works)
- Take away all packaging for responsible disposal

Our delivery team will NOT (as standard):
- Install or plumb in a washing machine or dishwasher
- Connect or disconnect gas appliances (legally requires a Gas Safe registered engineer)
- Install a built-in or integrated appliance
- Remove or dispose of your old appliance (see recycling and removal service below)
- Mount a TV on a wall (see VoltInstall below)
- Connect to your home network, streaming services, or external devices

UPPER FLOOR DELIVERY
Standard delivery covers up to the first floor (one flight of stairs). If your property
requires delivery above the first floor — for example, a flat on the third floor of a
building without a lift — you must declare this at checkout or when booking.
An upper floor surcharge applies: £25 per additional floor above the first.
If the building has a lift large enough to accommodate the product, no surcharge applies —
notify us at the time of ordering so the delivery team can prepare accordingly.
If access is not as declared (e.g. no lift where one was indicated, or more floors than
stated), the delivery team may be unable to complete delivery and a redelivery will be
booked. A failed delivery charge of £25 may apply if access was inaccurately described.

ACCESS REQUIREMENTS
Please ensure the delivery route is clear: from the street or car park, through your
front door, hallway, and into the room where the product will go.
Minimum doorway width for standard white goods: 60cm (the product itself is 60cm wide —
the doors must be removable or the doorway wider than 60cm for the product to pass through).
Many internal doors in UK homes are 62–67cm wide — this is sufficient.
Narrow Victorian terraced houses with 57–59cm internal doors: the delivery team
cannot force a product through. Customers in this situation should measure their
narrowest doorway before ordering a standard-width appliance. Slimline (45cm wide)
washing machines and dishwashers are available as an alternative.
If the delivery team arrives and cannot complete delivery due to access issues:
A failed delivery charge of £25 applies. A redelivery can be booked at a new date.
If the customer decides the product is unsuitable due to access, a collection and full
refund will be arranged — no restocking fee applies for access-related returns.

VOLTINSTALL — OPTIONAL PAID INSTALLATION SERVICES

VoltInstall services can be added to your order at checkout or by calling 0333 400 7000
before your delivery date. Services cannot be added on the day of delivery.

Washing machine installation (£49):
- Disconnect existing washing machine (if present)
- Connect new machine to existing hot or cold fill plumbing
- Run and check a short cycle to confirm no leaks
- Requires an existing plumbing connection of the correct type

Dishwasher installation (£49):
- Same scope as washing machine installation

Integrated appliance installation (£89):
- Fitting of integrated washing machine or dishwasher behind a kitchen door panel
- Includes all connections and a functional test
- Customer must provide the door panel — Voltex does not supply cabinetry

Oven installation — electric (£59):
- Connection of freestanding or slot-in electric oven to existing cooker circuit
- Requires a suitable 32-amp or 45-amp cooker circuit — if this does not exist,
  an electrician must be engaged separately before installation

Oven installation — gas (not offered by Voltex):
- Voltex does not offer gas appliance installation. Gas connections must be made by
  a Gas Safe registered engineer. We can recommend finding a Gas Safe engineer at
  gassaferegister.co.uk. Gas ovens and range cookers are delivered and positioned
  by our team; connection is the customer's responsibility.

TV wall mounting (from £49):
- Standard mounting on a flat solid wall: £49
- Mounting with cable concealment through the wall: £89
- Mounting on a non-standard surface (brick, stone, plasterboard, steel stud):
  assessed on-site; additional charges may apply if specialist fixings are required

OLD APPLIANCE REMOVAL AND RECYCLING

Voltex is the UK's largest recycler of technology and appliances. We offer:
Free recycling drop-off: bring your old appliance to any Voltex store and we will
recycle it responsibly at no charge.
Collection with delivery (£20): our delivery team collects your old appliance on
the same visit as delivering your new one. The old appliance must be disconnected
and ready for removal before the delivery team arrives. We do not disconnect old
appliances as part of the removal service unless VoltInstall disconnection is purchased.
We accept: washing machines, fridge-freezers, dishwashers, ovens, TVs (any size),
computers, and most other electrical products. We do not accept gas appliances that
have not been safely disconnected by a qualified engineer.
NOTE: the old appliance removal service is separate from VoltInstall. If the customer
wants us to disconnect AND remove the old appliance, they need both the VoltInstall
disconnection service and the removal service — check this is added at checkout.

SMALL ITEM TRACKING

Once your order has been despatched, you will receive a despatch email with a tracking
link. Tracking is via the carrier's own website (Evri, DPD, or Royal Mail).
If the tracking link is not working or shows no updates:
- Allow up to 4 hours after the despatch email for tracking to activate
- If tracking shows no movement after 48 hours from despatch, contact us —
  we will investigate with the carrier

LARGE ITEM TRACKING

Your large item delivery can be tracked via voltex.co.uk/my-orders. The tracking page
shows the current status of your delivery:
Order confirmed → Preparing for despatch → With delivery team → Out for delivery → Delivered

On the day of delivery, you will receive an automated call and SMS when the delivery
team is approximately 30 minutes away. You do not need to wait in all day — once the
morning 1-hour window is sent, you have a clearer picture of when to be home.
If you need to leave before the delivery team arrives and a trusted adult can accept
delivery on your behalf, this is acceptable — ensure they are aware of what to check
on delivery (see Part 3).

PART 5 — FAILED AND MISSED DELIVERIES
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

SMALL ITEM MISSED DELIVERY

If you are not home when a courier attempts delivery:
The carrier will attempt delivery up to 3 times on different days (DPD and Evri),
or leave a card for collection at a local post office or collection point (Royal Mail).
After 3 failed attempts, the parcel is returned to Voltex. We will contact you to
arrange redelivery. A redelivery fee of £3.99 applies if the missed delivery was
not caused by a Voltex or carrier error.
Safe place and neighbour delivery: if you set a safe place or nominated neighbour
instruction via the carrier's app or tracking page, the carrier may leave the parcel
without a signature. Voltex and the carrier are not responsible for parcels left
in a safe place that are subsequently stolen or damaged — this instruction is at the
customer's own risk.
AGENT NOTE: if a customer says their parcel was left in a safe place they did not
authorise and it is missing, this is a carrier liability issue. Advise the customer
to raise a claim directly with the carrier in the first instance. If the carrier does
not resolve within 5 business days, Voltex Customer Relations will escalate on the
customer's behalf.

LARGE ITEM FAILED DELIVERY

If no one is home at the time of the large item delivery:
The delivery team will not leave large items unattended — this is for security and
liability reasons. They will attempt to call the number on the order.
If no contact is made, the delivery will be marked as failed and returned to our depot.
A new delivery date must be booked. A failed delivery charge of £25 applies if
the customer was simply not home and did not notify us in advance.
Failed delivery charges are waived if:
- Voltex or the delivery team was responsible for the failure (e.g. arrived outside
  the stated delivery window, incorrect address on our system)
- The customer had a genuine emergency and contacted us within 24 hours
- The product was visibly damaged on arrival and the customer refused delivery
  (refusing delivery of a damaged product is the correct action — see Part 6)

DELIVERY TO A DIFFERENT ADDRESS
If you know you will not be home, you can:
Request a neighbour delivery at checkout (name and house number required —
the neighbour's consent is your responsibility)
Request a safe place delivery (small items only — not available for large items)
Change the delivery address before despatch (see Part 7 on order amendments)
Rebook to a different date if your plans have changed (see Part 5, rebooking)

REBOOKING A LARGE ITEM DELIVERY

To change your large item delivery date:
Online: voltex.co.uk/my-orders — rebook up to 11:59pm the day before delivery
By phone: 0333 400 7000 option 1 — rebook up to 5pm the day before delivery
On the day of delivery: not possible — delivery cannot be rebooked once the team
is loaded and en route. If you need to cancel on the day, the failed delivery
charge of £25 applies.
Maximum rebooking: a delivery may be rebooked up to 2 times without charge.
A third rebook incurs a £25 administration fee.
NOTE: products are held at our depot for a maximum of 28 days from the original
delivery date. If delivery has not been completed within 28 days for reasons within
the customer's control, the order may be cancelled and refunded minus a £25 storage fee.

PART 6 — DAMAGED AND INCORRECT DELIVERIES
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

PRODUCT DAMAGED ON DELIVERY

If a large item is visibly damaged when the delivery team unpacks it:
Do not accept the delivery. Ask the delivery team to record the damage on their
delivery system and return the product. You do not need to keep a damaged product
to make a claim. Contact Voltex within 24 hours and we will arrange a replacement
delivery at no charge. Your original delivery slot priority is restored.

If damage is only discovered after the delivery team has left:
Contact us within 48 hours of delivery. Do not attempt to use the product —
take photographs of the damage before doing anything else. We will arrange a
free collection and replacement or full refund.
After 48 hours from delivery, damage discovered on large items is treated as
a potential use-related issue rather than a delivery fault. We will investigate,
but we cannot guarantee a free resolution beyond 48 hours. This is why we
strongly encourage customers to inspect products on delivery day.

For small items damaged in transit:
Contact us within 14 days of delivery. Take photographs. We will send a replacement
or process a refund. Return postage is free for carrier-damaged items — we provide
a prepaid returns label.

WRONG PRODUCT DELIVERED

If you receive a different product from what you ordered:
Contact us immediately. Do not open or use the incorrect product if possible.
We will arrange collection of the incorrect product and delivery of the correct one.
If the correct product is out of stock, you will be offered a suitable alternative
or a full refund. You will not be charged for the incorrect item under any circumstances.
NOTE FOR AGENTS: wrong product deliveries must be logged as a priority case in CRM
and flagged to the fulfilment team. If this is a large item, the next available
delivery slot should be offered at no additional charge.

MISSING ITEMS FROM A MULTI-ITEM ORDER

Large orders sometimes ship in multiple consignments. If only part of your order
has arrived, check your despatch email — items may have been split across multiple
deliveries with separate tracking references.
If the despatch email shows all items as sent but some have not arrived, contact us
after the expected delivery date has passed. We will investigate with the carrier.
If items are confirmed lost in transit, replacements are sent or a partial refund
is issued within 3 business days of the investigation being completed.

PART 7 — AMENDING OR CANCELLING YOUR ORDER
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

ORDER CANCELLATION

Online orders can be cancelled free of charge provided the order has not yet been
despatched. Once an order has been despatched, it becomes a return (see Repairs and
Returns policy).

How to cancel before despatch:
Online: voltex.co.uk/my-orders — cancel button available while order status shows
"Processing" or "Preparing for despatch"
By phone: 0333 400 7000 option 1. Cancellations by phone are possible until the
order is confirmed as despatched on our system.

How quickly we process cancellations:
The cancellation itself is immediate. Your refund is processed within 3 business days
of cancellation. Card refunds appear in your account within 3 to 5 business days
of processing.

CANCELLATION WINDOW BY ORDER TYPE

Small items (courier): cancellation is possible until the order shows "Despatched"
status. Once despatched, it is typically with the carrier within 2 to 4 hours
of the status change. If you miss the cancellation window, the item will arrive
and you can return it under our returns policy.

Large items: cancellation is possible up to 11:59pm the day before delivery.
On the day of delivery itself, cancellation is not possible. If you change your mind
and refuse delivery without prior notice, a failed delivery charge of £25 applies
and the product is returned to depot. A return will then be processed but the
£25 failed delivery charge is non-refundable.

VoltPay financed orders: cancellation cancels the finance agreement automatically
if done before despatch. After despatch, follow the VoltPay return process in the
Repairs and Returns policy.

CHANGING DELIVERY ADDRESS

For small items: possible up to the point of carrier collection (typically same day
or next morning after ordering). Contact us as soon as possible — address changes
cannot be made via the website and require a phone call to 0333 400 7000 option 1.
Once the carrier has collected the parcel, the address cannot be changed.

For large items: possible up to 11:59pm the day before delivery. Contact us on
0333 400 7000 option 1. Address changes may affect delivery date if the new address
falls in a different delivery zone or the slot is not available.
NOTE FOR AGENTS: address changes on large item orders require a manual update in
both the order management system and the delivery team booking system. This is a
two-step process — confirm both systems have been updated before closing the call.

ADDING OR CHANGING VOLTINSTALL SERVICES

VoltInstall services can be added or changed up to 48 hours before delivery.
Within 48 hours of delivery: no changes can be made as the delivery team will have
already been briefed and parts/equipment prepared.
On delivery day: VoltInstall services cannot be added. If the customer decides on
delivery day they want installation, they must book a separate VoltInstall visit
after delivery, which will incur the standard VoltInstall charge and be booked
for a future date.

PART 9 — COMMON DELIVERY QUESTIONS
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

Q: My tracking shows "delivered" but I have not received my parcel
A: First check: has it been left with a neighbour or in a safe place? Check around
the property and ask immediate neighbours. The carrier's tracking page may show a
photograph of where the parcel was left.
If you cannot locate the parcel: contact us within 14 days of the tracking showing
"delivered." We will raise a formal investigation with the carrier. Investigations
typically take 3 to 5 business days. If the parcel is confirmed as lost or
misdelivered, a replacement or full refund will be arranged.
NOTE FOR AGENTS: do not immediately promise a replacement on the first call —
the investigation process must be followed. However, if the customer has a compelling
account and the item is under £50, Team Leader can authorise an immediate replacement
pending investigation outcome.

Q: My washing machine was delivered but the engineer said they could not install it
because my plumbing is incompatible. What happens now?
A: This is a VoltInstall delivery outcome. The delivery team will have noted the
incompatibility on their system. Contact us to discuss options:
Option 1: arrange for a plumber to upgrade the connection, then rebook VoltInstall
at no additional charge (one free rebook allowed within 60 days).
Option 2: return the washing machine for a full refund — no restocking or failed
delivery charge applies when VoltInstall cannot complete due to a pre-existing
property issue. The machine will be collected on a booked date.

Q: I was told my delivery would arrive yesterday but it has not come
A: For small items: check the carrier tracking link in your despatch email.
Carrier delays do occur — most delayed parcels arrive within 1 to 2 additional
business days. If tracking shows no movement for more than 48 hours or shows
an error status, contact us and we will investigate with the carrier.
For large items: check voltex.co.uk/my-orders for status. If the status has not
updated and your delivery date has passed, call 0333 400 7000 option 1. Large item
delivery delays are rare but can occur due to vehicle issues or road incidents.

Q: Can I collect a large item the same day I order it?
A: If the product is in stock at your chosen store: yes, provided the store has
the warehouse capacity to hold it and the product is physically at that location.
Large items ordered online for click and collect at a store that holds the stock
can sometimes be ready same day. Call the store directly to confirm before ordering
— store contact numbers are on voltex.co.uk/stores.
If the product needs to transfer from a distribution centre: minimum 1 to 3 business days.

Q: I ordered two items together — one has arrived but the other has not
A: Multi-item orders sometimes ship in separate consignments. Check your despatch
confirmation email — if two separate tracking references are shown, each item is
tracked independently. If only one tracking reference is shown and only one item
has arrived, contact us — the second item may have been separated in the
carrier's sorting facility. We will investigate and confirm within 24 hours.

Q: I need my washing machine delivered urgently — my existing machine has broken
A: We understand this is a priority situation. Call 0333 400 7000 option 1 and
explain the urgency. Our team will check for the earliest available large item
delivery slot for your postcode. In some areas, next-day or 2-day large item
delivery is possible for urgent cases — this is not always shown online.
VoltCare Complete customers with an active VoltCare claim on a broken washing machine
are entitled to priority delivery on a replacement appliance — the VoltCare team
handles this separately from standard delivery booking.

AGENT QUICK DECISION GUIDE — DELIVERY ISSUES

Parcel shows delivered but not received → check safe place/neighbour first;
  if not found, raise carrier investigation (3–5 days); replacement authorised
  by TL for items under £50 if compelling account

Large item not delivered on booked date → check CRM for reason; confirm new date
  within 24 hours; waive rebook charge; escalate to fulfilment if no update

Customer wants to cancel → check despatch status first; if not despatched, cancel
  and refund; if despatched, process as return

Customer received damaged large item → log priority case; arrange collection and
  replacement; restore original slot priority

Customer wants to add VoltInstall on delivery day → not possible; book follow-up
  VoltInstall visit for future date at standard charge

Wrong product delivered → log priority in CRM; flag to fulfilment team; arrange
  collection of wrong item and delivery of correct item on next available slot

Address change request, small item → phone only, before carrier collection;
  confirm carrier has updated before closing call

Address change request, large item → update both order system AND delivery booking
  system; confirm both before closing call
